Our Foundation

A Vision Born
in 2019

Urban Bites began as a bold idea — what if Nigeria's rich culinary heritage could meet the precision of modern gastronomy? Founded in the heart of Abuja, we set out to honor the traditions of our grandmothers while embracing the future of food.

Every dish tells a story. Every ingredient is sourced from trusted local farmers. Every plate is a love letter to the city that inspires us every single day.

"Food is not just sustenance — it is memory, culture, and connection on a plate."

Chef Adaeze Nwosu

Urban Bites

Meet the Visionary

Chef Adaeze
Nwosu

Chef Adaeze Nwosu is a culinary innovator with over 15 years of experience in fine dining across Africa and Europe. Her approach to West African cuisine is rooted in respect for tradition while pushing the boundaries of contemporary gastronomy.

Trained under Michelin-starred chefs in Paris and Copenhagen, Adaeze returned to Nigeria with a mission: to elevate African cuisine to the world stage while celebrating the flavors of her homeland.

  • Culinary Training Le Cordon Bleu, Paris & Noma, Copenhagen
  • Awards Best New Restaurant 2020, African Culinary Excellence 2022
  • Philosophy Tradition meets innovation, local ingredients, global techniques
  • Passion Storytelling through food, community, and cultural preservation
