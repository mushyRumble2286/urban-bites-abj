Policies

Reservation Policies

Cancellation & Changes

Reservations can be modified or cancelled up to 48 hours before your booking. Cancellations made within 48 hours may incur a charge equal to 50% of the estimated bill. No-shows will be charged the full estimated amount.

Dress Code

Smart casual attire is required. For special experiences like Chef's Table, business casual or formal wear is recommended. We reserve the right to refuse entry for inappropriate attire.

Dining Duration

Standard dining experiences are approximately 2.5 hours. Chef's Table experiences last 3.5 hours. We request that tables be vacated by closing time to accommodate our team.

Special Requests

We accommodate dietary restrictions, allergies, and special requests. Please inform us at the time of booking or call us in advance to discuss your needs with our culinary team.
